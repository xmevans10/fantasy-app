# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 4, 2026_

BallIQ ("the app") is a sports-trivia game. This policy explains what data the app collects,
why, and how it's used. BallIQ does not sell your data or use third-party advertising trackers.

## Information we collect

**Account.** When you sign in with Sign in with Apple, we receive an Apple-generated user
identifier, and — only if you choose to share them — your name and email address (or Apple's
private relay email).

**Profile.** A username, optional avatar, and your primary sport preference.

**Gameplay data.** Your ratings and rating history, XP, streaks, and puzzle completion history
— what powers your rank, streaks, and league standings.

**User-generated content.** If you create and publish a Community puzzle, its content and your
authorship are stored and shown to other players. If you report a puzzle, the report (which
puzzle, which reason, your account) is stored for moderation.

**Push notifications.** If you enable notifications, we store a device push token and your
per-category notification preferences (streak reminders, league position, Versus challenges,
season-end).

**Usage analytics.** A small set of in-app events (e.g. onboarding completed, sign-in
completed, game started/completed, puzzle published, community puzzle played, share tapped,
report filed) tied to your account ID when signed in, or recorded anonymously when signed out.
These are used only to understand feature usage and are never sold or shared with advertisers.

## How we use this information

Solely to operate the app: authenticate you, track your rating/progress, power Leagues and
Versus matchmaking, show and moderate Community content, deliver the push notifications you've
opted into, and understand which features are used so we can improve the app.

## Who we share it with

We use Supabase (database/backend hosting) and Apple (Sign in with Apple, push notification
delivery) as infrastructure providers to operate the app. We do not sell data to, or share it
with, advertisers or other third parties.

## Data retention & deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. To request deletion of your account and
associated data, email **xmevans10@gmail.com**.

## Children's privacy

BallIQ is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal
information from children under 13.
